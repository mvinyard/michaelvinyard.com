Space: Boston Public Garden

Posted: Tuesday, September 3rd, 2019 - Michael Vinyard

Sunday was a beautiful day, so a friend and I got coffee and took a walk around the Boston Public Gardens. I've never actually noticed how well-kept a park this is. I also didn't know that in late summer, you might be able to find some really aesthetically-pleasing bugs on nearly every flower you look at.

I've gotten really into being a fraud of a photographer with my iPhone lately. I know absolutely nothing about photography or what makes a good photo, but lately I have been trying to take photos wherever I can in hopes of being able to decorate my apartment (or even the background of my phone screen) with things that have personal meaning.

You should tweet at me if you have any feedback on these photos; I'd love to hear it. I know absolutely nothing and I have fun trying to get a shot or two when the moment arises, especially if I am travelling.
